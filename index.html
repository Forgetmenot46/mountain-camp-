Mountain Camp

One activity that should not be missed in Various provinces in winter is camping in the middle of nature, surrounded by forests and streams.

Highly recommended for those escaping the urban jungle. Or find quality time with family or friends. At night it is very dark due to the lack of light pollution. and saw many stars across the sky
